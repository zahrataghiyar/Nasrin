Zahra Taghiyarrenani

Postdoctoral Researcher & Resident Researcher at Volvo Group

I develop machine learning methods for real-world conditions — studying heterogeneity across data sources, operating contexts, and temporal dynamics. My work spans domain adaptation, anomaly-aware forecasting, open-set recognition, and heterogeneous federated learning, with a strong emphasis on robustness and deployment in industrial settings such as predictive maintenance.
